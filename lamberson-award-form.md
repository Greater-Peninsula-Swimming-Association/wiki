## {{< meta gpsa.season >}} Essay Topic

> "How has your coach demonstrated joy, compassion and companionship in connecting with all swimmers on your team this summer?"

## About This Award

This is a **nomination award**, not an application. GPSA Representatives may nominate a coach and seek someone familiar with that coach to draft the nomination essay.

It is the intention of GPSA to award the Kei Lamberson Outstanding Coach Award to one coach or team of co-coaches per season. The Lamberson family has graciously committed to an annual contribution of **$500** to fund this award.

## Essay Requirements

- Minimum 250 words, maximum 750 words
- Submit as Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or similar format
- Attach to this completed nomination form

## Submission

Submit this completed nomination form and essay to your team's GPSA Representative.

**Deadline:** All entries must be emailed by the GPSA Representative to [scholarships@gpsaswimming.org](mailto:scholarships@gpsaswimming.org) with subject "Kei Lamberson Coach Award" by **midnight, {{< meta gpsa.scholarship-deadline >}}**.

The winner will be announced at the Championship Meet on {{< meta gpsa.city-meet-date >}}.
